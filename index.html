Sneakers mandatory. Comfortable is the whole point.

Arrive at noon opening. Plan 90 min to 2 hrs. Steam rooms, cold plunge, repeat. The platza oak leaf scrub is the classic move if anyone's feeling brave. The most iconic sweating of your life.

Optional: High Line Walk
Elevated park built on a former railway line, running from Gansevoort St up to 34th St on the west side of Manhattan. Great views, public art, food vendors, Hudson River glimpses. Can do the full length (~1.5 miles) or just a stretch. Drops you right near Hell's Kitchen and Chelsea — convenient before heading to Atlas later.
Easy, scenic, no agenda. Perfect if you want to move around before a long night of standing at bars.
Free High Line entrance ↗ 🚇 A/C/E to 14th St, or L to 8th Ave

— 🍕 Pizza Options —
All GF-friendly options noted. Allie's covered no matter where the group lands.
Kesté Pizza & Vino ⭐ Best sit-down
271 Bleecker St, West Village
The gold standard for celiac-safe pizza in NYC. Dedicated GF kitchen, separate pizza cutters, every pie available GF with dairy-free options too. Also genuinely excellent pizza for everyone. Takes reservations — book ahead for a group.
GF ✓ Dedicated kitchen 271 Bleecker St ↗
Emily
35 Downing St, West Village
Husband-and-wife spot, celeb favorite. Famous for the Colony pie — white pizza, pickled cherry peppers, honey. GF options available. Good for a sit-down group dinner.
GF options ✓ 35 Downing St ↗
Don Antonio
309 W 50th St, Hell's Kitchen
45 different pies, every one available GF. Good option if you're still in the HK zone before heading downtown. No reservations needed.
GF ✓ Every pie 309 W 50th St ↗
Joe's Pizza ⭐ Quick slice stop
7 Carmine St, West Village
The most iconic slice in NYC. Thin, foldable, perfect. Cash only, no frills. Fast and cheap — ideal for a group slice stop between bars. Not GF.
Not GF Cash only 7 Carmine St ↗
Prince Street Pizza
27 Prince St, Nolita
Famous pepperoni squares — crispy, caramelized, spicy. Line is long but moves fast. Slight detour from the West Village but worth it if the group is hungry. Not GF.
Not GF 27 Prince St ↗
Rubirosa
235 Mulberry St, Nolita
Classic NYC red sauce joint. GF crust on every pie plus GF apps — calamari, arancini, mozzarella sticks. Staff very knowledgeable about cross-contamination. Good backup if Kesté is full.
GF ✓ + GF apps 235 Mulberry St ↗
